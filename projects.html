Hosting

I made a Raspberry Pi 4 cluster which I'm hosting my projects on. Is it more performant than hosting on AWS or Azure? Probably not. Is it cheaper? Definitely not. Is it more secure? I imagine not, but I tried! Does it offer flexibility that I could not otherwise get? Sure, but I'm not really doing anything with that flexibility. But it did make me learn more about Linux servers, K8s, Docker, networking, etc. than hosting on a cloud provider. And isn't learning the real purpose of personal projects anyway?
